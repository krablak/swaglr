Welcome to SWAGLR

Swaglr is a simple tool for sharing small bits of web pages with each other.

It is unsophisticated. Swaglr enables you easily share (or quote as we say) any piece of interesting information you find online.

Swaglr helps you quote selected text, images, links and whole pages. Just make a selection in your browser, right click and send it to Swaglr.
