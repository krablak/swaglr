Welcome to SWAGLR!

Long time we were missing a simple service for sharing small bits of web with each other.

Something usable, sexy and unsophisticated. Something what enables easily share any piece of interesting information we find online.

So we created Swaglr.

Swaglr helps you quote selected text, images, links and whole pages. Just make a selection in your browser, right click and send it to Swaglr.
